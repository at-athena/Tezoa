About Tezos

Tezos is a cutting-edge blockchain platform known for its innovation in self-amending cryptographic technology. Unlike traditional blockchains, Tezos can evolve by upgrading itself, a feature that significantly reduces the risk of hard forks and enhances long-term upgradability. It employs a unique on-chain governance system where stakeholders have a say in the direction and modifications of the protocol. Tezos is also distinguished by its use of a Proof-of-Stake (PoS) consensus mechanism, making it more energy-efficient than platforms based on Proof-of-Work (PoW). This blockchain is designed to facilitate smart contracts and decentralized applications (dApps), providing a robust and flexible infrastructure for developers and users looking for a secure, sustainable, and participatory digital ecosystem.
